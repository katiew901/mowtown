About Us

Mow Town Landscaping is a family owned and operated landscaping service company headquartered in Worcester, MA. Mow Town Landscaping was founded by the Smith brothers in 1997. Since its inception, the company has been known for the quality and care put into every job. Mow Town services Central Massachusetts and Northern Rhode Island and Connecticut.
